Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scholario?
Scholario is a modern learning management system built exclusively for and operated on behalf of SHS Academy in Rawalpindi, Punjab, Pakistan.
Who is eligible to use Scholario?
Access is limited to enrolled students, parents/guardians, teachers, and staff members affiliated with SHS Academy.

Payers transfer school fees directly to SHS Academy bank accounts and submit payment references inside the portal for manual verification.
